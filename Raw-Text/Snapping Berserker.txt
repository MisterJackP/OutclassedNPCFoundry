Snapping Berserker
Medium humanoid, chaotic neutral
Armor Class 13 (natural armor)
Hit Points 67 (9d8 + 27)
Speed 40 ft., swim 40 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
16 (+3) 16 (+3) 17 (+3) 9 (-1) 16 (+3) 9 (-1)
Damage Resistances nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing
Skills Perception +5, Stealth +5, Survival +5
Senses passive Perception 15
Languages any one language
Challenge 3 (700 XP)
Amphibious. The berserker can breathe air and water.
Reckless. At the start of its turn, the berserker can gain advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls during that turn, but attack  rolls against it have advantage until the start of its next turn.
Actions
Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 25 (2d8 + 2d12 + 3) piercing damage.
