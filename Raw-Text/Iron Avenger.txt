Iron Avenger
Medium humanoid (any race), any alignment
Armor Class 25 (iron avenger suit)
Hit Points 204 (24d8 + 96)
Speed 30 ft., fly 60 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
14 (+2) 18 (+4) 18 (+4) 20 (+5) 15 (+2) 18 (+4)
Damage Resistances nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing
Skills Smith's Tools +11, Tinker's Tools +11
Senses blindsight 60 ft., darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 12
Languages any three languages
Challenge 20 (25,000 XP)
Evasion. When the iron avenger is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, it instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails.
Actions 
Multiattack. The iron avenger makes two attacks with its blasters, then uses its seeker missiles.
Blaster. Ranged Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, range 100/300 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d8 + 3) radiant damage.
Seeker Missiles. The iron avenger targets three creatures it can see within range of its blindsight. Each target takes 10 (4d4) piercing damage. This damage can be blocked with the shield spell.
