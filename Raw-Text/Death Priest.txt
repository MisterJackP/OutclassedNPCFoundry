Death Priest
Medium humanoid (any race), any alignment (usually
evil)
Armor Class 13 (chain shirt)
Hit Points 27 (5d8 + 5)
Speed 30 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
10 (+0) 10 (+0) 12 (+1) 13 (+1) 16 (+3) 13 (+1)
Skills Medicine +7, Persuasion +3, Religion +3
Senses passive Perception 13
Languages any two languages
Challenge 2 (450 XP)
Morbid Weapons. The priest's weapon attacks  are
magical. When the priest hits a creature with a melee
weapon attack it deals an additional 9 (2d8) necrotic 
damage (included in the attack).
Spellcasting. The priest is a 5th-level spellcaster. Its
spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 13, +5 to
hit with spell attacks). It has the following cleric spells
prepared.
Cantrips (at will): chill touch, spare the dying,
thaumaturgy
1st level (4 slots): false life, inflict wounds, ray of
sickness
2nd level (3 slots): blindness/deafness, ray of
enfeeblement
3rd level (2 slots): animate dead, vampiric touch
