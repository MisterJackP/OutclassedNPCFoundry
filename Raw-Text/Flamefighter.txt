Flamefighter
Medium humanoid (any race), any alignment
Armor Class 14 (hide armor)
Hit Points 165 (30d8 + 30)
Speed 30 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
10 (+0) 14 (+2) 13 (+1) 10 (+0) 18 (+4) 15 (+2)
Skills Nature +3
Senses passive Perception 14
Languages Druidic, Ignan, any one language
Challenge 8 (3,900 XP)
Binder of Fire Spirits. Creatures killed by fire damage originating from the flamefighter burst into spectral flames upon death. These spectral flames immediately reconstitute into a wildfire spirit under the flamefighter's control.
Spellcasting. The flamefighter is a 9th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting  ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). It has the following druid spells prepared.
Cantrips (at will): control flames, create bonfire, fire bolt, produce flame
1st level (4 slots): absorb elements, burning hands, create or destroy water, entangle
2nd level (3 slots): locate animals or plants, heat metal, scorching ray
3rd level (3 slots): dispel magic, fireball, plant growth
4th level (3 slots): aura of life, fire shield, wall of fire
5th level (1 slots): conjure elemental (fire elemental only), flame strike, raise dead
Actions
Flamefighter Fan. The flamefighter waves its fan. Each creature in a 10-foot cube originating from the flamefighter must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be pushed 10 feet away. Objects up to 50 pounds are also pushed, and nonmagical fires are extinguished.
Reactions
Fiery Teleportation. When a wildfire spirit under the flamefighter's control uses its Fiery Teleportation, the flamefighter can also teleport to an unoccupied space within 30 feet of it that it can see.
