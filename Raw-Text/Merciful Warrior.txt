Merciful Warrior
Medium humanoid (any race), any alignment
Armor Class 15
Hit Points 52 (7d8 + 21)
Speed 40 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
10 (+0) 17 (+3) 16 (+3) 10 (+0) 14 (+2) 10 (+0)
Senses passive Perception 12
Languages any one language
Challenge 2 (450 XP)
Hands of Harm. The warrior's unarmed strikes are magical and deal an additional 7 (2d6) necrotic damage on a hit (included in the attack). If the warrior hits a poisoned or incapacitated creature with an unarmed strike, it deals an additional 7 (2d6) necrotic damage.
Noxious Aura. As a bonus action, the warrior can activate or deactivate its noxious aura. While active, creatures that start their turn within 5 feet of the warrior must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned until the end of the warrior's next turn.
Unarmored Defense. While the warrior isn't wearing armor, its armor class includes its Wisdom modifier.
Actions
Multiattack. The warrior makes two unarmed strikes.
Unarmed Strike. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) necrotic damage.
