Note of Potential
Tiny construct, neutral
Armor Class 15
Hit Points 1 (1d4 - 1)
Speed 0 ft., fly 90 ft. (hover)
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
1 (-5) 20 (+5) 8 (-1) 3 (-4) 3 (-4) 3 (-4)
Damage Immunities all damage except force
Condition Immunities blinded, charmed, deafened, exhaustion, frightened, incapacitated, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, stunned, unconscious
Senses passive Perception 6
Languages understands the languages of its creator but can't speak
Challenge 1/8 (25 XP)
Conductor-Bound. The note of potential shares initiative with its conductor, can occupy its space, and can move up to 90 ft. outside of initiative order to always remain within 5 feet of the conductor.
Ephemeral. The note of potential can't take actions, and it can only take reactions  if it is within 5 feet of its conductor. After taking a reaction, or if it ends its turn further than 5 feet from its conductor, the note of potential is destroyed.
Reactions
Note of Destruction. When the conductor makes an attack roll, the note of potential emits a burst of sound. Each creature within 5 feet of it that can hear it must must make a Constitution saving throw or take 4 (1d8) thunder damage.
Note of Ingenuity. When the conductor makes an ability check, the note of potential grants it advantage on that check.
Note of Protection. When the conductor takes damage, the note of potential grants it resistance to the damage of that attack.
