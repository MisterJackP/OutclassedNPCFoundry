Valor Bard
Medium humanoid (any race), any alignment
Armor Class 18 (scale mail, shield)
Hit Points 88 (16d8 + 16)
Speed 30 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
14 (+2) 14 (+2) 12 (+1) 10 (+0) 13 (+1) 14 (+2)
Saving Throws Dex +4, Wis +3
Skills Acrobatics +4, Perception +5, Performance +8
Senses passive Perception 15
Languages any two languages
Challenge 4 (1,100 XP)
Spellcasting. The bard is a 4th-level spellcaster. Its
spellcasting  ability is Charisma (spell save DC 12, +4 to
hit with spell attacks). It knows the following bard
spells.
Cantrips (at will): blade ward, mending, vicious
mockery
1st level (4 slots): bane, dissonant whispers, heroism,
sleep, thunderwave
2nd level (3 slots): enhance ability, heat metal
Song of Rest. The bard can perform a song while taking
a short rest. Any ally who hears the song regains an
extra 1d6 hit points if it spends a Hit Dice to regain hit
points at the end of that rest. The bard can confer this
benefit on itself as well.
Actions
Multiattack. The bard makes two attacks with its
longsword.
Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5
ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 2) slashing damage, or 7
(1d10 + 2) slashing damage if used with two hands to
make a melee attack. 
Reactions
Combat Inspiration. When an attack roll is made against
the bard or a friendly creature the bard can see within
60 feet of it, the bard can add 4 (1d8) to the target's AC
against that attack.
