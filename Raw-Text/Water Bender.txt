Water Bender
Medium humanoid (any race), any alignment
Armor Class 13 (leather armor)
Hit Points 22 (4d8 + 4)
Speed 35 ft., swim 35 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
10 (+0) 14 (+2) 12 (+1) 10 (+0) 14 (+2) 12 (+1)
Senses passive Perception 12
Languages Primordial, any one language
Challenge 2 (450 XP)
Actions
Water Whip. Melee Spell Attack: +4 to hit, reach 30 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d10 + 2) bludgeoning damage, and the target must make a DC 11 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 22 (4d10) bludgeoning damage and is knocked prone or pulled up to 25 feet toward the bender (bender's choice). On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage and isn't pulled or knocked prone.
