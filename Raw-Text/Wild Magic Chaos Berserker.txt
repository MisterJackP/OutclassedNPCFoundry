Wild Magic Chaos Berserker
Medium humanoid, chaotic neutral
Armor Class 13 (hide armor)
Hit Points 67 (9d8 + 27)
Speed 30 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
16 (+3) 12 (+1) 17 (+3) 9 (-1) 14 (+2) 9 (-1)
Damage Resistances nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing
Senses passive Perception 12
Languages any one language
Challenge 4 (1,100 XP)
Reckless. At the start of its turn, the berserker can gain advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls during that turn, but attack rolls against it have advantage until the start of its next turn.
Wild Magic Initiative. When the berserker rolls initiative, it rolls twice on the Wild Magic Surge table and uses either result. If it rolls the same number twice, it can choose any result on the table.
Actions
Multiattack. The berserker makes two melee weapon attacks. 
Greataxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d12 + 3) slashing damage.
Weave-Infused Axe. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (1d12 + 6) force damage. This is a +3 magic weapon that returns to the berserker's hand attack.
Reactions
Wild Magic Reaction. When the berserker takes damage, it rolls twice on the Outclassed Wild Magic Barb Alt table and uses either result. If it rolls the same number twice, it can choose any result on the table.
