Infiltrator Armorer
Medium humanoid, any alignment
Armor Class 21 (power armor plate mail, shield)
Hit Points 55 (10d8 + 10)
Speed 35 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
11 (+0) 14 (+2) 15 (+2) 17 (+3) 12 (+1) 10 (+0)
Skills Smith's Tools +5, Stealth +4
Senses passive Perception 11
Languages any two languages
Challenge 3 (700 XP)
Initiate Spellcasting. The armorer is a magic initiate. Its
spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 13, +5
to hit with spell attacks). It can cast the following
wizard spells.
At will: mending, shocking grasp
1/day: shield
Actions
Multiattack. The armorer makes two attacks  with its
lightning launcher.
Lightning Launcher. Ranged Spell Attack: +5 to hit,
range 90/300 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (2d6) lightning
damage, and the next attack roll against the target
from a creature other than the armorer before the end
of the armorer's next turn is made with advantage.
