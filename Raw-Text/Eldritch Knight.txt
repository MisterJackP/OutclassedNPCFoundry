Eldritch Knight
Medium humanoid (any race), any alignment
Armor Class 18 (chain mail, shield)
Hit Points 105 (14d8 + 42)
Speed 30 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
18 (+4) 11 (+0) 17 (+3) 14 (+2) 10 (+0) 10 (+0)
Skills Arcana +5, Athletics +7
Senses passive Perception 10
Languages any two languages
Challenge 6 (2,300 XP)
Bonded Weapon. The knight's bonded weapon is its
javelin. The eldritch knight cannot be disarmed if it is
wielding its javelin. As a bonus action, the knight can
magically teleport its javelin into its hand.
Spellcasting. The knight is a 2nd-level spellcaster. Its
spellcasting  ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 13, +5
to hit with spell attacks). It knows the following wizard
spells, which it can cast without somatic or material
components.
Cantrips (at will): prestidigitation, ray of frost
1st level (3 slots): alarm, shield, unseen servant
Actions
Multiattack. The knight makes two attacks with its
javelin.
