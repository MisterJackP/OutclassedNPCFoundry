Beastmaster
Medium humanoid (any race), any alignment
Armor Class 15 (hide armor)
Hit Points 75 (10d8 + 30)
Speed 30 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
12 (+1) 15 (+2) 16 (+3) 10 (+0) 18 (+4) 12 (+1)
Skills Animal Handling +8, Survival +6
Senses passive Perception 14
Languages any one language
Challenge 2 (450 XP)
Animal Companion. The beastmaster is bonded to a
beast, which is its animal companion. The companion
acts on its own inititative, but otherwise obeys the
beastmaster's commands. As a bonus action, the
beastmaster can command its companion to use its
reaction  to take the Dash, Disengage, Dodge, or Help
action.
Actions
Multiattack. The beastmaster makes two weapon
attacks.
