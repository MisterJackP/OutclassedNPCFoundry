Fey Wanderer
Medium humanoid (any race), neutral
Armor Class 15 (studded leather)
Hit Points 121 (22d8 + 22)
Speed 40 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
10 (+0) 18 (+4) 12 (+1) 10 (+0) 19 (+4) 14 (+2)
Skills Perception +7, Performance +5, Persuasion +5
Condition Immunities charmed, frightened
Senses passive Perception 17
Languages Sylvan plus any one language
Challenge 7 (2,900 XP)
Enchanting Strike (1/Turn). A creature hit by the fey wanderer's attack must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened or charmed of the fey wanderer (fey wanderer's choice) until the end of its next turn.
Magic Weapons. The fey wanderer's weapon attacks are magical and deal an additional 14 (4d6) psychic damage on a hit (included in the attack).
Actions
Multiattack. The fey wanderer makes three attacks  with its scimitars or two attacks  with its longbow.
Scimitar. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 4) piercing damage plus 14 (4d6) psychic damage.
Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing damage plus 14 (4d6) psychic damage.
