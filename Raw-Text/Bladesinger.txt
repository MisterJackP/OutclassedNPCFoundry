Bladesinger
Medium humanoid, any alignment
Armor Class 21 (studded leather)
Hit Points 78 (12d8 + 24)
Speed 40 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
10 (+0) 20 (+5) 15 (+2) 18 (+4) 13 (+1) 15 (+2)
Saves Con +5
Skills Acrobatics +8, Perception +4, Performance +8
Senses darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13
Languages any three languages
Challenge 5 (1,800 XP)
Bladesong. While the bladesinger isn't wearing Medium
or Heavy armor or using a shield, its AC includes its
Intelligence modifier, and it has advantage on
Constitution saving throws made to maintain
concentration on a spell.
Spellcasting. The bladesinger is a 5th-level spellcaster. It
spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 15, +7
to hit with spell attacks). It has the following wizard
spells prepared.
Cantrips (at will): gust, mending, prestidigitation, ray
of frost
1st Level (4 slots): color spray, expditious retreat, false
life, find familiar, shield
2nd Level (3 slots): cloud of daggers, hold person,
mirror image, misty step, see invisibility, spider climb
3rd Level (2 slots): counterspell, fly, haste
Victorious Blades. The bladesinger's melee weapon
attacks are magical, and it adds its Intelligence modifier
to the weapon's damage (included in the attack). The
bladesinger can also use its Dexterity to wield one-handed weapons that aren't heavy.
Actions
Multiattack. The bladesinger makes two melee weapon attacks.
Scimitar. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (1d6 + 9) slashing damage.
Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (1d8 + 9) slashing damage.
Handaxe. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (1d6 + 9) slashing damage.
War Pick. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (1d8 + 9) piercing damage.
Whip. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (1d4 + 9) slashing damage.
